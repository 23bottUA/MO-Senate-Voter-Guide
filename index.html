MO Senate Voter Guide '22

About.

This voter guide is meant to inform current and future voters on US Senate candidates from Missouri and the values those candidates hold.

Party lines are not mentioned anywhere on this guide. (However, campaign websites for each candidate might.) This was done because voters should choose a candidate based on personal beliefs instead of party lines. The United States is currently too divided by political parties, so the parties were removed to try to introduce a solution to this issue.


AP United States Government and Politics Enduring Understanding Connections

  • MPA-3: Factors associated with political ideology, efficacy, structural barriers, and demographics influence the nature and degree of political participation.

    • Political ideology and beliefs are described in each candidate's bio to inform voters and encourage them to vote based on their own personal beliefs.

  • PRD-3: The various forms of media provide citizens with political information and influence the ways in which they participate politically.

    • Media, such as this website and candidates' social media pages, inform voters of candidates' stances on certain issues and influence how they vote in elections.

Candidates.

Information came from various sources, including Ballotpedia, campaign websites, and social media pages. Not all candidates are completely open about issues they believe in, so some candidates may have less information under their names than others.
